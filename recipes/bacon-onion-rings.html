Bacon Onion Rings

Game Day must-have Onion Rings

These Bacon Onion Rings are full of smokey bacon flavor with a crispy Doritos crust, served with your favorite dipping sauce.

Ingredients:

Onion Rings

  • 1 Large White Onion
  • 12 stripes Bacon
  • 2 Eggs
  • 300 g Doritos

Sour Cream Dipping

  • 200 g Sour Cream
  • 50 g Fresh Chives
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

The Onion Bacon Onion Rings

  1. Preheat oven to 190Â°C degrees. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a nonstick baking mat and set aside. Beat eggs in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Add chips to a ziplock bag or food processor and pulverize into crumbs. Put crumbs into a small bowl.
  3. Peel onion and slice into large 2 cm rings. Wrap each ring tightly with two strips of bacon. Repeat for remaining rings.
  4. Brush rings with eggs. Then dredge each ring in crumbs and place onto prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes in the lower third of the oven. Remove from oven.

The Dipping

  1. Mix the sour cream with the chopped chives
  2. Season it with salt and pepper
  3. Serve the finished rings with dipping sauce and enjoy the game!
